Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2, 2025

Your privacy is important to us at GeodeticExam. This policy explains briefly what information we handle when you use our website.

What Information We Handle

  • Optional Name: You can enter your name to see it on the results page. This is optional and stored only temporarily in your browser (`sessionStorage`) during your visit. We don't save it on our servers.
  • Exam Data: While you take an exam, your questions and answers are stored temporarily in your browser (`sessionStorage`) so we can calculate your score and let you review them. This is cleared when you close the browser tab.
  • Usage Info: We might collect general usage data (like browser type, pages visited) using tools like Firebase Analytics to help us improve the site. This is usually anonymous.
  • Theme Preference: We save your light/dark mode choice in your browser (`localStorage`) so the site remembers it next time.
  • Admin Login: If you access the admin panel, login details are processed (ideally securely via Firebase Authentication, though currently using a demo method).

How We Use Information

We use this information mainly to:

  • Run the practice exams and show you your results.
  • Allow you to review your answers after an exam (during the same session).
  • Personalize your results page slightly if you provide your name.
  • Understand how the site is used so we can make it better (using anonymous data).
  • Operate and secure the website and admin functions.

Sharing Information

We don't sell or rent your optional name.

  • We use Firebase (Google) for storing exam questions, images, and potentially for anonymous analytics. Google has its own privacy policy for how it handles data.
  • We might share information if required by law or to protect the site and users.
  • We might share anonymous, summarized usage data for analysis.

Data Security

We take steps to protect the site, but no online service is 100% secure. Remember that your optional name and exam answers are stored temporarily in your browser.

Your Rights

You have rights regarding your data under privacy laws like the Philippines Data Privacy Act. Since we store very little personal data, the main thing is understanding how we use temporary browser storage and Firebase. You can clear browser storage (`sessionStorage`, `localStorage`) yourself.

Changes to This Policy

We might update this policy. If we do, we'll change the "Effective Date" at the top.
