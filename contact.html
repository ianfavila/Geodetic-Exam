Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I subscribe to the Geodetic Engineering Reviewer?

    You can subscribe to our platform by clicking on the "Subscribe" button in the top right corner of our homepage. Follow the instructions to create an account and select your preferred subscription plan.

  • What payment methods do you accept?

    We accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, GCash, and bank transfers. You can select your preferred payment method during the checkout process.

  • How often is the content updated?

    We update our content regularly to ensure it remains accurate and relevant. Major updates are typically done quarterly, with minor updates and corrections implemented as needed.

  • Can I access the platform on my mobile device?

    Yes, our platform is fully responsive and can be accessed on any device including smartphones and tablets. We also offer a mobile app for Android and iOS devices for enhanced offline access.

  • How do I report a technical issue?

    You can report technical issues by sending an email to support@geodeticreviewer.com with details about the issue you're experiencing. Our support team will respond within 24 hours.
